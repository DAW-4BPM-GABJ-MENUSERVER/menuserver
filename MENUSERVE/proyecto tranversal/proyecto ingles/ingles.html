¡INTRODUCTION TO THE TOPIC!


The overcrowding of houses in Tijuana
can lead to looking for more land for
build them but since there is no more space
some people decide to invade places
such as parks, preventing
recreation and sports spaces.

In this project we will create a house
ecological, with recycled materials
in order to help the environment.
An ecological house is one that achieves
optimal habitability conditions
with minimal energy consumption, having
take into account the orientation of the construction
the terrain and the nature that surrounds it.

How would you support the development of green homes? The topic of green
homes may be new to many of us, but it is important to know that they arise due
to recent climate changes. Starting from “Rural Schools” the idea of ​​adopting the project to create
ecological houses with which families who currently live in a deplorable way can benefit
due to the lack of economic resources that prevent them from obtaining decent housing since
for this we would simply need the natural resources that we can expropriate from the specific place in which it would be carried
out. said action. For the reasons mentioned above, an ecological house design will be studied which would have the purpose
of improving the quality of life and at the same time being friendly to the environment.

There are great challenges and one of the main ones
pis to ensure that at least the
majority of families have access to decent housing and in optimal conditions. For the community in the
urban sector, due to its constant process and opportunities, it is easier for them
to obtain a home in extremely good conditions. optimal for living with dignity, otherwise for the rural sector
where economic marginalization is the main factor why families cannot obtain their own home.
